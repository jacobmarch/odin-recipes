Lasagna Recipe

Description

A gooey and delicious dish for an entire family to enjoy. Lasagna is a main course that can feed the whole family, and it takes very little preparation time compared to other dishes. This dish is filled with cheese, sauce, and meat in order to create the perfect blend of flavor and consistency.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
