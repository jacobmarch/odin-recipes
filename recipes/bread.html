Bread Recipe

Description

This is the instruction manual to create a wonderful loaf of bread for any purpose including sandwiches, sliced bread or as a topping in another dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water(110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Preparation

  1. Place water, sugar, salt, oil, bread flour and yeast into pan of bread machine.
  2. Bake on White Bread setting. Cool on wire racks before slicing.
